EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK - HR POLICIES

1. LEAVE POLICY
===============

1.1 Annual Leave
- Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per year
- Leave must be approved by your manager at least 2 weeks in advance
- Unused leave can be carried forward to the next year (maximum 5 days)

1.2 Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to 10 days of sick leave per year
- Medical certificate required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days
- Sick leave cannot be carried forward

1.3 Maternity/Paternity Leave
- Maternity leave: 12 weeks paid leave
- Paternity leave: 2 weeks paid leave
- Additional unpaid leave available upon request

2. WORKING HOURS
================

2.1 Standard Hours
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- 1-hour lunch break (12:00 PM - 1:00 PM)
- Flexible working hours available with manager approval

2.2 Overtime
- Overtime is paid at 1.5x regular rate
- Maximum 10 hours overtime per week
- Overtime must be pre-approved by manager

3. BENEFITS
===========

3.1 Health Insurance
- Comprehensive health insurance coverage
- Dental and vision included
- Coverage for dependents available

3.2 Retirement Benefits
- 401(k) matching up to 6% of salary
- Vesting period: 3 years
- Company contribution: 3% of salary

3.3 Professional Development
- Annual training budget: $2,000 per employee
- Conference attendance support
- Certification reimbursement

4. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
=========================

4.1 Performance Reviews
- Annual performance reviews conducted in December
- Mid-year check-ins in June
- 360-degree feedback for senior positions

4.2 Performance Improvement
- Performance improvement plans for underperforming employees
- 90-day improvement period
- Regular check-ins during improvement period

5. DISCIPLINARY PROCEDURES
==========================

5.1 Progressive Discipline
- Verbal warning
- Written warning
- Final written warning
- Termination

5.2 Immediate Termination
- Theft or fraud
- Violence or threats
- Drug or alcohol use at work
- Gross misconduct

6. EQUAL OPPORTUNITY
====================

6.1 Non-Discrimination
- Equal opportunity employer
- No discrimination based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability
- Harassment-free workplace

6.2 Accommodations
- Reasonable accommodations for disabilities
- Religious accommodations
- Pregnancy accommodations

7. CONFIDENTIALITY
==================

7.1 Company Information
- All company information is confidential
- Non-disclosure agreements required
- Intellectual property protection

7.2 Employee Privacy
- Personal information kept confidential
- Limited access to personnel files
- Privacy in workplace communications

8. SAFETY AND SECURITY
======================

8.1 Workplace Safety
- Safety training required for all employees
- Incident reporting procedures
- Emergency evacuation plans

8.2 Security
- ID badges required for building access
- Visitor registration required
- Computer and data security policies

9. COMMUNICATION
================

9.1 Internal Communication
- Company-wide emails for important announcements
- Intranet for policy updates
- Regular team meetings

9.2 Grievance Procedures
- Informal resolution with manager
- Formal grievance filing with HR
- Appeal process available

10. TERMINATION
===============

10.1 Resignation
- 2 weeks notice required
- Exit interview conducted
- Return of company property

10.2 Termination
- Severance pay based on years of service
- COBRA continuation available
- Final paycheck within 3 business days
